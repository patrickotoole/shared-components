It's called momentum advertising. And it'll blow your mind.

Rockerbox targets users based on their real time intent. We analyze the recent browsing activity of each user to immediately classify the behavior of their current browsing session. We buy media based on the user behavior that has the greatest likelihood to respond to your ad.

Your audience is more than when or where. We uncover why.

Today, advertisers are buying their audience is bought based on content or historical visits. Rockerbox understands audiences by looking at user behavior. We consider all the places a user visits in a single browsing session and use this classification to optimize towards the behavior that performs best for your offer. No more guessing where your audience visits or when they'll convert. Start targeting why user's respond and get better results.

Big data. Smart data. Fast data.

Our technology analyzes information on over 1 million users every 7 seconds to classify the behavior of their session. We use this behavioral information to optimize our media purchasing, ensuring that the user's behavior matches the advertiser's message.

Only serve ads to real users. (Bots don't buy your product)

A recent survey estimates that fraud accounts for 40% of the impressions in online advertising. Another study claims 60% of all internet traffic is performed by bots. User's respond to ads — bots don't. Our technology only targets users with intent, meaning you don't waste your money on artificial eyeballs.
